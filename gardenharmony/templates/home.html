About Justin Durango, founder of Garden Harmony

I am a local horticulturist, designer, and garden artist in the greater Raleigh area. I help local landowners cultivate beautiful, functional habitats.

My intention is to make the greater Raleigh area more biologically diverse, healthy, artful, and efficient, one gardener at a time.

My services include:

  • Helping excited novice gardeners and homeowners learn the fundamentals of gardening and horticulture
  • Introducing concepts of design that will allow the gardener to lay out the most balanced and productive space possible for themselves
  • Producing landscape designs for homeowners that elegantly utilize patterns from the natural world and produce beautiful, low-maintenance gardens, and balance the needs for human space with wildlife
  • Organizing workshops, on site, for homeowners (and friends/family/neighbors) covering topics from arbor construction to growing zucchini
